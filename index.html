About Us

Lumos Studio is an information technology solutions company that aims to provide simple, high-impact solutions that meet the needs of clients, big and small, across all corporate sectors in Nigeria.

Our mission is to bring the benefits of Information Technology to the small business owner and all else in need of these solutions. We try to do this by being empathetic to our clients' needs while being sensitive to their budgets.
